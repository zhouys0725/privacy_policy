Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how 拾音 Vois collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the application. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

This page is intended to serve as the public privacy policy for product pages, app review, and end users.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Capitalized words have the meanings defined below, whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • Application means the 拾音 Vois software product.
  • Company means 拾音 Vois and is also referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our".
  • Device means any device used to access the Service, including phones, tablets, or computers.
  • Personal Data means information relating to an identified or identifiable person.
  • Service Provider means any third party processing data on behalf of the Company.
  • Usage Data means technical and diagnostic information collected automatically through use of the Service.

Collecting Personal Data

Data We May Collect

  • Phone number, where required for account or contact purposes.
  • Usage data, including IP address, browser type, operating system details, device identifiers, access times, and interaction logs.
  • Location information, if permission is granted.
  • Camera and photo library data, if permission is granted to support product features.
  • AI feature input data, including voice recordings or audio streams you actively submit for speech recognition, the transcribed text derived from that audio, and related analysis results generated to support the feature.

Permission-Based Access

Location, camera, and photo library access are requested only when needed for product functionality. You can revoke these permissions at any time through device settings.

AI Service Inputs

When you use AI-powered speech recognition and analysis features, we collect the voice recordings or audio streams you actively provide, the transcribed text derived from that audio, and related analysis results in order to call third-party AI interfaces and deliver speech recognition, language analysis, and related core functions.

How We Use Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To manage accounts, support requests, and product operations.
  • To contact you about updates, security notices, and service-related matters.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve product performance.
  • To prevent abuse, protect infrastructure, and meet legal obligations.
  • To support business transfers such as mergers, acquisitions, or restructurings if required.
  • To invoke third-party AI interfaces when you actively use AI functions, so as to provide speech recognition results and related analysis outputs.

Retention of Personal Data

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Account-related data: during the account lifecycle and up to 24 months after closure.
  • Usage analytics and logs: generally up to 24 months for troubleshooting, security, and product improvement.
  • Extended retention: may apply for legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup handling, or fraud prevention.

Transfer of Personal Data

Your information may be processed in locations where the Company or its service providers operate. Where applicable law requires it, we will use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Deleting Your Data

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data you have provided to us.

Some information may be retained if we have a legal basis or legitimate operational need to keep it.

Disclosure of Personal Data

  • To service providers that help operate, support, or improve the Service.
  • In connection with business transactions such as merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or valid governmental request.
  • When necessary to protect rights, property, public safety, or the integrity of the Service.
  • To third-party AI technology providers when you choose to use speech recognition and analysis features. In order to provide those services, we transmit your input content, including voice audio and related text data, to Alibaba DAMO Academy (Hangzhou) Technology Co., Ltd. That provider acts as our data processor and processes the data only in accordance with our instructions for the purpose of completing speech recognition and analysis.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect personal data, but no transmission or storage method is completely secure. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to support and improve the Service.

  • FreshDesk: customer support software operated by Freshworks, Inc. Privacy policy: freshworks.com/privacy
  • AI technology provider: when you use speech recognition and analysis features, your submitted inputs, including voice audio and related text data, may be transmitted to Alibaba DAMO Academy (Hangzhou) Technology Co., Ltd.. We collect your voice recordings or audio streams, the transcribed text derived from that audio, and related analysis results for the purpose of calling third-party AI interfaces to provide speech recognition, language analysis, and related core features. The provider acts as our data processor and processes the data only under our instructions to complete the requested recognition and analysis. We require the provider to apply contractual data protection measures, including encryption and restrictions consistent with your privacy settings, such as not using the submitted content to train its base model where such commitments are available. You may choose not to use AI features, and doing so will not affect your access to other core functions of the application.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 16. If we learn that personal data from a child under 16 has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When material changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions, requests, or privacy concerns, contact us at zhouys0725@gmail.com.

解释与定义

解释

定义

  • 应用 指拾音 Vois 软件产品。
  • 公司 指拾音 Vois，在本政策中亦称“我们”。

  • 服务提供商 指代表我们处理数据的第三方服务机构或个人。
  • 使用数据 指用户在使用服务过程中自动产生的技术、日志和诊断信息。

我们收集的信息

个人信息及相关数据类型

  • 手机号码：在注册、登录、身份核验、通知联系或客户支持等场景中，可能依法依约收集。
  • 使用数据：包括 IP 地址、浏览器类型、操作系统信息、设备标识符、访问时间、页面停留情况及操作日志等。
  • 位置信息：仅在您授权后，为实现特定功能或优化服务体验而收集。
  • 麦克风权限及音频数据：仅在您授权后，为实现语音采集、语音识别及语音分析功能而访问您的麦克风，并处理您主动提交的语音音频数据。
  • AI 功能相关数据：当您使用 AI 语音识别及分析功能时，我们将收集您主动提交的语音音频、由该音频转换形成的文本内容，以及与识别、分析相关的结果数据。

基于授权的权限访问

位置信息、麦克风等系统权限，仅会在相关功能确有必要时向您申请。您可以随时通过设备系统设置撤回相关授权， 但撤回授权后，部分依赖该权限的功能可能无法正常使用。

关于 AI 服务输入内容的特别说明

当您使用本应用提供的 AI 语音识别及分析功能时，我们将收集您主动提交的语音音频、由语音转换形成的文本内容以及相关分析结果。 收集上述数据的目的，在于调用第三方 AI 接口，以实现语音识别、语音内容分析等核心功能。

  • 用于防范欺诈、滥用、非法使用及履行法律法规规定的义务。
  • 在发生合并、收购、重组、资产转让或类似交易时，用于支持必要且合法的业务交接安排。
  • 在您主动使用 AI 功能时，用于调用第三方 AI 接口，以实现语音识别、语音内容分析，并向您返回相应的识别结果和分析结果。
