Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how 拾音 Vois collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the application. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

This page is intended to serve as the public privacy policy for product pages, app review, and end users.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Capitalized words have the meanings defined below, whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • Application means the 拾音 Vois software product.
  • Company means 拾音 Vois and its operator, Beijing Gravity Arc Technology Center, and is also referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our".
  • Device means any device used to access the Service, including phones, tablets, or computers.
  • Personal Data means information relating to an identified or identifiable person.
  • Service Provider means any third party processing data on behalf of the Company.
  • Usage Data means technical and diagnostic information collected automatically through use of the Service.

Collecting Personal Data

Data We May Collect

  • Account and authentication data, including phone number, username, password hash, login tokens, refresh tokens, and SMS verification information where required for account registration and login.
  • Voice diary and AI data, including voice recordings or audio streams you actively submit, transcript text generated from the audio, diary entries, titles, moods, summaries, insights, next-step suggestions, extracted items, and weekly review content.
  • Subscription and payment-related data, including subscription status, product identifier, order number, transaction status, and payment result needed to verify entitlements and provide after-sales support.
  • Settings and notification data, including reminder preferences, weekly reminder time, and related trigger information.
  • Usage and device data, including IP address, browser type, operating system details, device identifiers, access times, and necessary interaction logs for security and troubleshooting.

Tracking Disclosure

If you authorize tracking on Apple platforms, device identifiers and user-related identifiers may be collected and used for tracking purposes as required by the platform privacy disclosure framework. Where such authorization is requested, you may decline it in the system prompt or later change your choice in device settings.

Permission-Based Access

Microphone permission is requested when needed for voice recording and voice diary creation. You can revoke this permission at any time through device settings, but voice recording and related AI functions may then be unavailable.

AI Service Inputs

When you use AI-powered speech recognition and analysis features, we collect the voice recordings or audio streams you actively provide, the transcribed text derived from that audio, your diary content, and related analysis results in order to call third-party AI interfaces and deliver speech recognition, diary summarization, insight extraction, and weekly review generation.

How We Use Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To manage account registration, login, identity verification, and support requests.
  • To create, store, and display voice diary records and history.
  • To contact you about updates, security notices, and service-related matters.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve product performance.
  • To prevent abuse, protect infrastructure, and meet legal obligations.
  • To synchronize subscription status, process payment-related states, and provide membership benefits.
  • To provide reminder settings and weekly reminder scheduling.
  • Where platform authorization is granted, to use certain identifiers for tracking-related purposes disclosed in the relevant app store privacy labels.
  • To support business transfers such as mergers, acquisitions, or restructurings if required.
  • To invoke third-party AI interfaces when you actively use AI functions, so as to provide speech recognition, structured summaries, insights, and weekly review outputs.

Retention of Personal Data

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Account-related data: during the account lifecycle and up to 24 months after closure.
  • Voice diary, transcript, summary, and weekly review data: generally retained during the account lifecycle unless you delete the relevant content or close your account, subject to lawful retention requirements.
  • Subscription and payment-related data: retained as required to verify entitlements, handle disputes, process refunds, and comply with financial, tax, or platform obligations.
  • Usage analytics and logs: generally up to 24 months for troubleshooting, security, and product improvement.
  • Extended retention: may apply for legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup handling, or fraud prevention.

Transfer of Personal Data

Your information may be processed in locations where the Company or its service providers operate. Where applicable law requires it, we will use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Deleting Your Data

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data you have provided to us.

Some information may be retained if we have a legal basis or legitimate operational need to keep it.

Disclosure of Personal Data

  • To service providers that help operate, support, or improve the Service.
  • To SMS verification service providers where needed to send login or verification codes.
  • To Apple App Store, WeChat Pay, and other payment or subscription service providers as needed to create orders, verify purchases, and confirm entitlements.
  • In connection with business transactions such as merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or valid governmental request.
  • When necessary to protect rights, property, public safety, or the integrity of the Service.
  • Where platform authorization is granted, certain device identifiers or user-related identifiers may be processed for tracking-related purposes reflected in the corresponding platform privacy disclosures.
  • To third-party AI technology providers when you choose to use speech recognition and diary analysis features. In order to provide those services, we transmit your voice audio, transcript text, and related diary content to Tongyi Yunqi (Hangzhou) Information Technology Co., Ltd. That provider acts as our data processor and processes the data only in accordance with our instructions for the purpose of completing speech recognition, summarization, insight generation, and weekly review analysis.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect personal data, but no transmission or storage method is completely secure. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to support account authentication, AI processing, subscription services, payments, and overall operation of the Service.

  • AI technology provider: when you use speech recognition and diary analysis features, your submitted inputs, including voice audio, transcript text, and related diary content, may be transmitted to Tongyi Yunqi (Hangzhou) Information Technology Co., Ltd.. We collect your voice recordings or audio streams, the transcribed text derived from that audio, diary content, and related analysis results for the purpose of calling third-party AI interfaces to provide speech recognition, structured summaries, insights, and weekly review outputs. The provider acts as our data processor and processes the data only under our instructions to complete the requested recognition and analysis. We require the provider to apply contractual data protection measures, including encryption and restrictions consistent with your privacy settings, such as not using the submitted content for unrelated purposes.
  • Payment and subscription service providers: when you purchase memberships or subscriptions, payment-related data may be processed by Apple App Store, WeChat Pay, and other applicable payment channels. We receive and process only the information necessary to verify purchase status, activate benefits, and handle refunds or support.
  • SMS verification service providers: when phone verification is required, your phone number and necessary verification request data may be processed by SMS service providers solely for sending verification codes and preventing abuse.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 16. If we learn that personal data from a child under 16 has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When material changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions, requests, or privacy concerns, contact us at zhouys0725@gmail.com.
